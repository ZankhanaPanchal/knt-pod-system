How this works

Every POD submitted here — details and photos — is written straight to a Google Sheet that dispatch uses as their live dashboard. It's shared only with dispatch, never public.
  • Each ticket is one row: driver, dates, load numbers, locations, notes, and links to the photos.
  • Photos are automatically converted to JPG and saved to a linked Google Drive folder — works the same whether you snap a new photo or choose one from your gallery.
  • To find or fix a POD, use "Update an existing POD" above with the ticket number — it works from any phone, not just the one you submitted from.
  • Ask dispatch for the Sheet link if you ever need to double-check a submission.
